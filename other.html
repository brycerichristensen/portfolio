Other Rocketry Projects

I have designed, manufactured, and launched multiple high-power rockets, which have given me experience with simulation software, avionics, composites, and more. Check them out below!

Bomb Pop

Advanced launch competition rocket using an L1000 motor. Dual-deploy with live telemetry and onboard camera. Achieved an apogee of 8,055 feet AGL, landing only 55 feet from the target. Placed 1st in the competition.

Legatus

Tripoli L2 certification rocket powered by a J270 motor. Fabricated using carbon fiber with a custom avionics bay. Dual-deploy with onboard camera. I hand-soldered the flight computer (including SMT) and integrated the avionics for a robust flight system.

Praetor

Tripoli L1 certification rocket using an H135 motor. Capable of live telemetry and equipped with an onboard camera for flight data capture.

1776

Student launch competition rocket powered by a G74 motor. Placed 3rd out of approximately 25 teams.
