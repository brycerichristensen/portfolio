Compliant Mechanism Mechanical Battery

As a payload engineer for BYU’s High Power Team, I am developing a 3U CubeSat payload—a compliant mechanism mechanical battery—for the 2026 SDL payload challenge. The project involves materials research and geometric optimization, with iterative prototyping and FEA used to ensure robust energy storage and structural integrity during flight.
